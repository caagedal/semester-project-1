Events

Welcome to our Museum Events page, where science and discovery come to life with a variety of exciting experiences.

All events are free of charge, but events with limited space will require you to sign up.

Here's a glimpse of our upcoming events

ongoing event

dinodiscovery: unearth the past

Journey back in time with "DinoDiscovery," an ongoing exhibition that reveals the wonders of the Jurassic era. Encounter lifelike dinosaur replicas, authentic fossils, and engaging talks from experts. Become a paleontologist with interactive digs and step into the Mesozoic era with our time-travel theater. Join us in uncovering the mysteries of Earth's ancient past!

COMING NEXT MONTH

ENERGETICA EXHIBITION

Discover the power of the elements with our Energetica exhibition on loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam. Immerse yourself in installations showcasing the magic of harnessing solar and wind energy, shedding light on sustainable power sources.

COMING THIS NOVEMBER

Night in the museum

Bring your family for an unforgettable night surrounded by dinosaurs and science displays. Grab your sleeping bags and prepare to rough it as we embark on a fascinating exploration of science during this special overnight adventure..

Sign up for the event at the front desk in the museum

COMING THIS NOVEMBER

VISITING PROFFESSOR OF AERONAUTICS

Join us as we welcome Prof Sheila Widnall from MIT for three captivating lectures on the future of aeronautics. Explore the evolving world of 'space' and the groundbreaking developments in this field.

Sign up for the lectures at the front desk in the museum

Stay tuned for more exciting events each month, as we continue to bring you new and diverse experiences that celebrate the wonders of science. Join us on this journey of discovery!
