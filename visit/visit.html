Visit us

Admission

The museum is free of charge to enter

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 150 NOK per person, you pay at the entrance.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Our special events require their own tickets, you can find a schedule of our events and where to buy tickets here.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Contact

Contact us on email or phone if you have any questions, we ususally answer within 24 hours in weekdays.
